Yu cu 100% cn b, cng chc cp x phi c BNG I HC t 01/08/2023

Bo Lao ng
Ngy xut bn:
13.11.2024

Ngh nh s 33/2023/N-CP ca chnh ph ban hnh mt s quy nh mi v cn b, cng chc cp x v nhng ngi hot ng khng chuyn trch cp x, thn, t dn ph. 

  • Ton b cng chc cp x c bng i hc

Ngh nh s 33 ca chnh ph, c hiu lc t ngy 01/8/2023 quy nh r tiu chun ca tng chc danh cng chc cp x nh sau:

  • Tiu chun cng chc Ch huy trng Ban Ch huy Qun s cp x thc hin theo quy nh ca php lut chuyn ngnh v qun s. Theo Thng t lin tch 01/2013/TTLT-BNV-BQP yu cu Ch huy trng phi c bng tt nghip trnh trung cp chuyn nghip ngnh qun s c s tr ln.
  • Tiu chun ca cng chc Vn phng thng k; a chnh xy dng th v mi trng (i vi phng, th trn) hoc a chnh nng nghip xy dng v mi trng (i vi x); Ti chnh k ton; T php h tch; Vn ha x hi nh sau:

tui: 18 tui tr ln;

Trnh gio dc ph thng: Tt nghip trung hc ph thng;

Trnh chuyn mn nghip v: Tt nghip i hc tr ln ca ngnh o to ph hp vi yu cu nhim v ca tng chc danh cng chc cp x.

Nh vy, ngh nh quy nh r tiu chun v khung nng lc ca cng chc cp x l t i hc tr ln (tr trng hp lut hoc iu l ca t chc c quy nh khc). 

Cng theo quy nh chuyn tip ti iu 38 Ngh nh 33/2023/N-CP, i vi cc cn b, cng chc cp x cha p ng tiu chun th trong thi hn 5 nm k t ngy ngh nh c hiu lc (01/8/2023) phi p ng tiu chun theo quy nh. Trong trng hp ht thi hn m cha p ng tiu chun th s thc hin ch ngh hu (nu iu kin) hoc thc hin chnh sch tinh gin bin ch theo quy nh ca Chnh ph.

  • Nhng trng hp cng chc cp x KHNG bt buc c bng i hc

C ba trng hp ngoi l khng bt buc cng chc cp x phi c bng i hc:

Th nht, trng hp php lut c c quy nh khc 

Chng hn ti iu 72 Lut H tch 2014 c quy nh cng chc t php h tch cp x phi c cc tiu chun sau y:

C trnh t trung cp lut tr ln v c bi dng nghip v h tch;

C ch vit r rng v trnh tin hc ph hp theo yu cu cng vic.

Do , i vi chc danh cng chc t php h tch, hin nay ch yu cu c trnh t trung cp lut tr ln m khng bt buc phi c bng i hc.

Th hai, trng hp cng chc cp x lm vic ti cc a bn kh khn

Ch yu cu trnh chuyn mn, nghip v t trung cp tr ln i vi cng chc cp x lm vic ti cc x, phng, th trn thuc a bn min ni, vng cao, bin gii, hi o, x o, vng su, vng xa, vng ng bo dn tc thiu s v vng c iu kin kinh t x hi c bit kh khn. 

Th ba, quy nh chuyn tip ti iu 38 Ngh nh 33/2023/N-CP 

Nh cp trn, quy nh ny nu r trng hp cn b, cng chc cp x ang gi chc danh quy nh m cha c bng i hc s c thi gian 5 nm p ng tiu chun theo quy nh. Nu qu thi hn 5 nm k t ngy 01/8/2023 th s xy ra 2 trng hp: Ngh hu (nu iu kin) hoc tinh gin bin ch theo quy nh ca chnh ph. 

  • Cn b, cng chc c th s dng bng i hc trc tuyn b sung h s 

Ti Vit Nam, bng i hc trc tuyn c cng nhn c gi tr tng ng vi bng i hc chnh quy. iu ny c quy nh ti Lut Gio dc i hc s 34/2018/QH14, c hiu lc t ngy 01/07/2019.

Bn cnh , B Gio dc v o to ban hnh Thng t 27/2019/TT-BGDT cho php khng ghi hnh thc o to nh chnh quy, ti chc, t xa trn vn bng i hc m ch ghi trn ph lc vn bng, c hiu lc t ngy 1/3/2020. 

Nh vy, bng i hc trc tuyn c gi tr php l v gi tr s dng nh bng i hc chnh quy. Cn b, cng chc tt nghip i hc trc tuyn c th s dng bng cp ca mnh b sung h s, phc v cng tc.

Hc i hc theo hnh thc trc tuyn rt ph hp i vi cc cn b, cng chc nhng ngi bn rn, khng sp xp c thi gian hc c nh. Vi hnh thc ny, cc cn b c th hc tp ti nh, ch ng sp xp thi gian hc theo lch trnh c nhn khng nh hng n cng vic. Ngoi ra, phng thc tuyn sinh ca i hc trc tuyn ch gm mt hnh thc l xt tuyn, gip cn b cng chc gim bt p lc n thi, d dng tip cn vi chng trnh hc.
